About SignalWave FX

SignalWave FX is a free, live forex trading signals channel. Every signal we publish includes the currency pair, trade direction, entry price, stop loss, and take profit level, so you can see exactly what's being suggested and decide whether it fits your own strategy.

Signals are posted in real time under Active Signals at the top of this page, and update automatically as our team publishes new trades and moves closed ones to the archive. Alongside the signals, our newsroom feed shares short updates on market-moving events as they happen.

Forex trading carries risk, and signals on this channel are provided for informational purposes only, not as financial advice. Always do your own research and consider your own risk tolerance before trading.
